## Purpose

This Business Operating System (BOS) is the institutional backbone of **FINBERG ADVISORY OÜ**, an Estonian professional consulting firm registered under **EMTAK 70201 — Business and Other Management Consultancy Activities**.

It is the single, integrated set of manuals that defines how the firm thinks, sells, delivers, manages risk, and scales. It is written to the standard of Tier-1 consulting firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Deloitte, PwC) and is intended to be used by:

- Founders and shareholders
- Executives and partners
- Managers and consultants
- New hires and contractors
- External auditors and investors

## Regulatory Positioning

FINBERG ADVISORY OÜ is a **non-regulated management consultancy**. It does **not** provide:

- Investment services or investment advice as defined under MiFID II
- Banking or credit institution services
- Payment services or e-money services
- Custody or safekeeping of client funds
- Portfolio management or order execution
- Insurance or insurance distribution

All work product, sales material, and contracts must consistently reflect this scope.

## Document Conventions

- **MUST / SHALL** — mandatory
- **SHOULD** — strong recommendation; deviation requires written justification
- **MAY** — optional
- **RACI** — Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed
- **KPI** — Key Performance Indicator
- **SOP** — Standard Operating Procedure

## Change Control

All material changes to any manual require approval from the Executive Committee and are logged in the Document Control register at the end of each manual.
